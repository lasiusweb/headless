# Initial Concept
KN Biosciences — Headless E-Commerce Platform (NestJS + Next.js 15 + Flutter POS)
Architecture includes a NestJS backend, Next.js storefronts, and a Flutter POS system.

# Product Definition - KN Biosciences Headless E-Commerce

## Vision
To build a high-performance, scalable, and resilient headless e-commerce ecosystem that bridges the gap between KN Biosciences and its diverse customer base, from large-scale B2B distributors to individual rural farmers.

## Target Users
- **B2B Dealers and Distributors:** Primarily focused on bulk ordering, role-based pricing, and credit management.
- **B2C Agricultural Customers (Farmers):** Requiring an accessible, mobile-friendly interface for individual product purchases.
- **Internal Administrators and Warehouse Staff:** Managing the catalog, inventory, order approvals, and logistics.

## Key Features (MVP)
- **B2B Portal:** Secure login for dealers with negotiated pricing, volume-based discounts, and simplified bulk order forms.
- **Offline-First Flutter POS:** A mobile application designed for 48-hour autonomy in low-connectivity rural areas, supporting barcode scanning and local stock caching.
- **B2C Storefront:** A modern Next.js 15 application providing a seamless shopping experience with GST-compliant checkout.
- **Unified NestJS Backend:** A modular monolith architecture providing a single source of truth for inventory, pricing, and orders.

## Brand Identity
- **Professional & Corporate:** Establishing B2B trust through a clean, reliable, and authoritative visual presence.
- **Accessible & Community-Focused:** User interfaces designed for clarity and ease of use, even for users with limited technical proficiency.
- **Modern & Tech-Driven:** Utilizing cutting-edge "headless" architecture to ensure lightning-fast performance and future-proof scalability.

## Critical Integrations
- **Zoho Ecosystem:** Automated synchronization of invoices and sales orders with Zoho Books, and lead management with Zoho CRM.
- **Payment Gateways:** Robust integration with Easebuzz and PayU for secure online payments.
- **Logistics & Shipping:** Real-time rate calculation and tracking via Delhivery and VRL logistics.

## Business Objectives
- **Digital Transformation:** Moving away from manual order processing to an automated, error-free digital workflow.
- **Market Expansion:** Opening direct-to-consumer channels to reach farmers directly.
- **Inventory Precision:** Achieving real-time stock visibility across all warehouses and mobile points of sale.
