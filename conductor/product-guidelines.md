# Product Guidelines - KN Biosciences

## Prose Style & Tone
- **Educational & Supportive:** For B2C farmers, use clear, step-by-step guidance and helpful tips regarding product usage and agricultural benefits.
- **Efficient & Transactional:** For B2B dealers and POS users, prioritize brevity and speed. Information should be scannable and task-focused.
- **Authority & Trust:** Maintain a professional and scientific tone in corporate and technical sections to reinforce KN Biosciences' expertise.

## Accessibility (A11y)
- **Outdoor Optimization:** Support high-contrast modes and adjustable font sizes to ensure readability under direct sunlight.
- **Localization:** Provide full support for regional languages (Telugu, Kannada) to bridge the language gap for rural users.
- **Touch-Friendly UI:** Use large touch targets (min 44x44px) and a minimalist layout to facilitate ease of use on mobile POS devices.

## Design Principles
- **Performance First:** All storefronts must target a sub-2.5 second page load on 4G mobile networks.
- **Mobile-First approach:** Design primarily for the smartphone experience, ensuring all features are fully functional on small screens before scaling up.
- **Semantic Integrity:** Use standard HTML elements correctly to ensure SEO optimization and compatibility with assistive technologies.

## Feedback & Error Handling
- **Action-Oriented Messaging:** Never present an error without a suggested next step or solution.
- **Diagnostic Clarity:** Include specific error codes or identifiers to help technical support resolve issues quickly.
- **Resilience:** Design the UI to degrade gracefully; if a non-critical service (like a recommendation engine) fails, the core shopping flow should remain intact.

## Visual Assets
- **SVG Graphics:** Use SVGs for all icons and logos to maintain sharpness at any resolution with minimal file size.
- **Next-Gen Image Formats:** Serve all product imagery in WebP format with appropriate resizing to balance quality and load times.
- **Illustrative Assistance:** Incorporate clear, representational icons alongside text to assist users with varying literacy levels.
